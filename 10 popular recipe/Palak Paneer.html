Ingredients

For the Curry:

  • 200g paneer (cubed)
  • 2 bunches of spinach (palak)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 green chili
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Spinach

  1. Wash spinach thoroughly and blanch in boiling water for 2 minutes.
  2. Transfer to cold water immediately, then blend with green chili into a smooth puree.

Step 2: Cook the Masala

  1. Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds, then chopped onions. SautÃ© until golden.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
  3. Add tomato puree, turmeric, coriander powder, and salt. Cook till oil separates.

Step 3: Combine & Simmer

  1. Add spinach puree and cook for 4â€“5 minutes on medium flame.
  2. Add paneer cubes and garam masala. Stir gently and simmer for another 5 minutes.
  3. Add cream (if using), mix well and turn off the heat.

Serve With
