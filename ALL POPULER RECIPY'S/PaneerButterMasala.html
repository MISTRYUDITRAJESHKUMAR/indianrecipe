🧈 Paneer Butter Masala Recipe

Ingredients

Main Ingredients:

  • 200g paneer (cubed)
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • ½ tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp sugar (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2–3 tbsp fresh cream
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions

Step 1: Sauté Base

  1. Heat oil and 1 tbsp butter in a pan. Add bay leaf and chopped onions. Sauté until golden.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook until raw smell goes away.
  3. Add tomato puree, red chili powder, turmeric, salt, and sugar. Cook until oil separates.

Step 2: Make the Gravy

  1. Add ½ cup water and simmer for 5 minutes.
  2. Add paneer cubes, garam masala, and the remaining 1 tbsp butter.
  3. Mix gently and simmer for 3–4 minutes.
  4. Add fresh cream, stir and cook for 1 minute. Turn off heat.

Step 3: Garnish & Serve

  1. Garnish with chopped coriander and a swirl of cream.
  2. Serve hot with naan, roti, or jeera rice.

Serve With

  • Butter naan or garlic naan
  • Jeera rice or plain basmati rice
  • Green salad or onion rings
🍽️ Tip: Soak paneer in warm water for 10 mins before adding to gravy for soft texture.
