🥔 Masala Dosa Recipe

Ingredients

For Dosa Batter:

  • 2 cups rice
  • ½ cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • ½ tsp fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Water for soaking and grinding

For Potato Masala (Filling):

  • 3–4 boiled potatoes
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1–2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • ½ tsp turmeric powder
  • 8–10 curry leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tsp oil

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Batter

  1. Soak rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds in water for 4–6 hours.
  2. Grind to a smooth batter, add salt, and let it ferment overnight or 8–12 hours.

Step 2: Make the Potato Masala

  1. Heat oil, add mustard seeds, curry leaves, and green chilies.
  2. Add onions and sauté till soft. Add turmeric.
  3. Mash boiled potatoes and add to the pan. Mix well with salt.
  4. Cook for 3–5 minutes and keep aside.

Step 3: Make the Dosa

  1. Heat a non-stick tawa or iron skillet. Grease lightly.
  2. Pour a ladle of batter and spread into a thin circle.
  3. Drizzle some oil around edges and cook until golden brown.
  4. Place some potato masala in the center and fold the dosa.

Serve With

  • Coconut chutney
  • Sambar
  • Tomato chutney
🔥 Tip: For crisp dosas, the pan should be hot but not smoking. Wipe with a wet cloth between dosas.
