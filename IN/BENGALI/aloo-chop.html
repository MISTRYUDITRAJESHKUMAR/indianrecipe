Ingredients

  • 4 medium-sized potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tbsp ginger, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup onions, finely chopped
  • 1 green chili, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh coriander leaves, chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions

  1. Heat a little oil in a pan and add cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  2. Add chopped ginger, onions, and green chili. SautÃ© until soft.
  3. Add turmeric, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Cook for 2 minutes.
  4. Mix this into the mashed potatoes along with chopped coriander leaves. Allow the mixture to cool.
  5. Shape the potato mixture into small oval-shaped balls.
  6. Roll them in breadcrumbs and set aside.
  7. Heat oil in a frying pan. Once hot, deep fry the potato chops until golden brown and crisp.

Serve With

  • Green chutney
  • Sweet tamarind chutney
  • Hot tea
