Ingredients

  • 4 medium-sized potatoes (peeled and cut into cubes)
  • 2 tbsp poppy seeds (posto)
  • 2 green chilies (slit)
  • 1/2 tsp nigella seeds (kalonji)
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp sugar (optional)
  • 1 tbsp mustard oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Grind the poppy seeds with a little water to form a smooth paste.
  2. Heat mustard oil in a pan. Add nigella seeds and slit green chilies. Let them splutter.
  3. Add the potato cubes and sautÃ© for a few minutes.
  4. Add turmeric powder and mix well. Cook for 5-7 minutes until the potatoes are slightly golden.
  5. Add the poppy seed paste, salt, and a little water. Cover and cook until the potatoes are tender and the flavors are well combined (about 10-15 minutes).
  6. Sprinkle sugar if using and mix well.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves before serving.

Serve With

  • Steamed rice
  • Roti or paratha
