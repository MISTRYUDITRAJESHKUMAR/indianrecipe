Ingredients

  • 2 large eggplants (brinjal)
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 tomato (chopped)
  • 2-3 green chilies (finely chopped)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tbsp mustard oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Roast the eggplants directly on an open flame or in the oven until the skin is charred and the flesh is soft.
  2. Allow the eggplants to cool, then peel off the skin and mash the flesh.
  3. Heat mustard oil in a pan, add cumin seeds and allow them to splutter.
  4. Add the chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  5. Add the ginger-garlic paste and saut√© for another minute.
  6. Add the chopped tomatoes, green chilies, turmeric, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until tomatoes soften.
  7. Add the mashed eggplant and stir well. Cook for a few more minutes.
  8. Sprinkle garam masala and mix well. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.

Serve With

  • Steamed rice
  • Roti or paratha
  • Fried fish
