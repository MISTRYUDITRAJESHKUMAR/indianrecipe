Ingredients

  • 1 large eggplant (brinjal), sliced into thin rounds
  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • Water to make batter
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix gram flour, turmeric, chili powder, baking soda, and salt.
  2. Add water gradually to make a smooth batter of medium consistency.
  3. Heat oil in a deep pan over medium heat.
  4. Dip eggplant slices in the batter, coat well, and fry until golden and crisp.
  5. Drain on paper towels to remove excess oil.

Serve With

  • Khichuri (Bengali-style khichdi)
  • Mustard sauce or kasundi
  • Hot tea as a snack
üí° Tip: Use fresh and firm eggplants for best texture and taste.
