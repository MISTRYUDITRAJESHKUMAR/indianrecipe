Ingredients

  • 1 cup chhena (fresh paneer)
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tsp rose water (optional)
  • 1 tsp ghee (for greasing)

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, knead the chhena until smooth. Add all-purpose flour, baking soda, and cardamom powder. Knead again for a minute until it forms a soft dough.
  2. Divide the dough into small portions and shape each portion into a smooth oval or cylindrical shape.
  3. Bring 2 cups of water to a boil in a deep pan and add sugar to make a syrup. Stir until the sugar dissolves completely.
  4. Once the syrup is ready, gently drop the shaped cham chams into the syrup. Let them cook in the syrup on medium heat for about 10-15 minutes, ensuring they puff up and absorb the sugar syrup.
  5. Remove the cham chams from the syrup and let them cool down.
  6. Optionally, you can add rose water to the syrup for a fragrant aroma.
  7. Serve chilled or at room temperature.

Serve With

  • Enjoy it as a sweet treat!
