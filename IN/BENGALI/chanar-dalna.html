Ingredients

For Chana‚Äôr Dalna (Chana Dal Curry):

  • 1 cup chana dal (split chickpeas)
  • 1 large onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tsp ginger paste
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala powder
  • 2 green chilies (slit)
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2 tbsp mustard oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Soak chana dal for at least 2-3 hours. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a pressure cooker, add chana dal with 3 cups of water and cook for about 4-5 whistles. The dal should be cooked but not mushy.
  3. Heat mustard oil in a pan, and add bay leaf and cumin seeds. Fry for a few seconds until aromatic.
  4. Add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  5. Add ginger paste, green chilies, and saut√© until the raw smell disappears.
  6. Add pureed tomatoes, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until the oil separates from the masala.
  7. Add the cooked chana dal to the pan, and mix well with the spices. Add some water to adjust the consistency of the gravy.
  8. Let the dal simmer for 5-7 minutes on low heat to allow the flavors to blend.
  9. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot.

Serve With

  • Steamed rice
  • Roti or paratha
