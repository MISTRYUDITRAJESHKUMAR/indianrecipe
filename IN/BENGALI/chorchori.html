Ingredients

  • 1 potato (peeled and cubed)
  • 1 carrot (sliced)
  • 1 eggplant (cubed)
  • 1 ridge gourd or bottle gourd (chopped)
  • 1 raw banana (cubed)
  • 1/2 cup green beans (cut into pieces)
  • 1 tsp panch phoron (Bengali 5-spice)
  • 1 dry red chili
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp mustard oil
  • 1/2 tsp sugar (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat mustard oil in a pan. Add panch phoron and red chili until aromatic.
  2. Add all chopped vegetables. Stir and mix well.
  3. Sprinkle turmeric, salt, and sugar. Stir to coat the veggies.
  4. Cover and cook on low heat, stirring occasionally, until vegetables are soft and lightly caramelized.
  5. Uncover and fry for a few minutes to let the bottom form a light crust ‚Äî the signature ‚Äúchorchori‚Äù.

Serve With

  • Steamed rice
  • Khichdi
