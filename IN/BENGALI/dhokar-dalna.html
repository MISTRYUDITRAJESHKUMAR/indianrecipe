Ingredients

For Dhokar (Lentil Cakes):

  • 1 cup yellow split peas (chana dal)
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/4 tsp asafoetida (hing)
  • 1 tsp ginger paste
  • 1-2 green chilies (finely chopped)
  • 1 tbsp rice flour
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying

For Gravy:

  • 2 tbsp mustard oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tbsp ginger paste
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Soak chana dal for 3-4 hours and grind into a coarse paste with ginger paste, green chilies, and water.
  2. Heat a pan, add cumin seeds and asafoetida. Pour the dal paste into the pan and cook for 10-12 minutes until the mixture thickens.
  3. Add rice flour and salt to the mixture. Stir and cook for another 2-3 minutes. Transfer the mixture to a greased plate and let it cool.
  4. Once cooled, cut the mixture into small squares or diamond shapes and fry the pieces in hot oil until golden brown. Set aside.
  5. For the gravy, heat mustard oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and ginger paste. Fry until aromatic.
  6. Add pureed tomatoes, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook the mixture for 5-7 minutes until the oil separates.
  7. Add water to adjust the consistency of the gravy. Bring to a boil.
  8. Gently add the fried dhokar pieces to the gravy and cook for another 5 minutes, allowing the flavors to blend.
  9. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot.

Serve With

  • Steamed rice
  • Roti or paratha
üí° Tip: You can add a pinch of sugar for extra sweetness to balance the spices.
