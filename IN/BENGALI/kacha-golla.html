Ingredients

  • 1 liter full cream milk
  • 1/2 tsp lemon juice or vinegar
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
  • 1 tbsp ghee (clarified butter)
  • Chopped pistachios and cashews for garnish

Instructions

  1. Boil the milk in a heavy-bottomed pan and reduce it to half its quantity, stirring continuously.
  2. Once the milk starts to thicken, add lemon juice or vinegar to curdle it. Stir gently until the curds separate from the whey.
  3. Drain the whey and keep the curds (chhena) aside. Allow it to cool for a few minutes.
  4. Heat ghee in a pan. Add the chhena and cook on low heat, stirring continuously, until it thickens and becomes soft.
  5. Add sugar and cardamom powder, and cook further until the mixture becomes a sticky dough-like consistency.
  6. Once the mixture is ready, shape it into small balls or oval shapes (Golla).
  7. Garnish with chopped pistachios and cashews.

Serve With

  • Serve Kacha Golla as a sweet dessert or enjoy it with your tea.
üí° Tip: You can also soak the Kacha Golla in sugar syrup for extra sweetness.
