Ingredients

  • 1 potato (peeled and cubed)
  • 1 carrot (sliced)
  • 1 eggplant (cubed)
  • 1/2 cup pumpkin (cubed)
  • 1/2 cup green beans (chopped)
  • 1/2 cup cauliflower florets
  • 1/2 cup raw banana (cubed)
  • 1 tsp ginger paste
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp mustard oil
  • 2 dried red chilies
  • 1/2 tsp sugar (optional)
  • 1/2 cup water (adjust as needed)

Instructions

  1. Heat mustard oil in a pot. Add cumin seeds and dried red chilies. Let them splutter.
  2. Add ginger paste and sautÃ© until fragrant.
  3. Add the mixed vegetables and stir well to coat with the spices.
  4. Sprinkle turmeric powder, cumin powder, garam masala, and salt. Stir to combine.
  5. Add water and cook on low heat until vegetables are tender, and the curry thickens.
  6. If using, add sugar to balance the flavors. Cook for a couple more minutes.

Serve With

  • Steamed rice
  • Roti
  • Fried fish
