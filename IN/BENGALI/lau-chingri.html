Ingredients

  • 1 medium bottle gourd (lau), peeled and diced
  • 200g small prawns, cleaned and deveined
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1-2 green chilies (slit)
  • 1/2 tsp ginger paste
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tsp sugar (optional)
  • 2 tbsp mustard oil

Instructions

  1. Marinate prawns with salt and turmeric. Set aside for 10 minutes.
  2. Heat mustard oil in a pan and lightly fry the prawns. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, add cumin seeds and green chilies. Saut√© for a few seconds.
  4. Add chopped bottle gourd and a pinch of salt. Cover and cook until soft.
  5. Add ginger paste, turmeric, sugar, and fried prawns. Mix well.
  6. Cook for a few more minutes until flavors combine. Add water if needed for a light gravy.

Serve With

  • Steamed rice
  • Light dal or masoor dal
