Ingredients

  • 500g Rohu or Katla fish (cut into steaks)
  • 2 potatoes (peeled and quartered)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 tsp ginger paste
  • 2‚Äì3 green chilies (slit)
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Mustard oil for cooking
  • Fresh coriander (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Marinate the fish with turmeric and salt. Set aside for 15 minutes.
  2. Heat mustard oil until it smokes. Fry the fish pieces until golden on both sides. Remove and set aside.
  3. Fry the potatoes until lightly browned and remove.
  4. In the same oil, add ginger paste, green chilies, tomatoes, and spices. Cook until tomatoes break down.
  5. Add potatoes and about 1.5 cups of water. Bring to a boil and simmer until potatoes are tender.
  6. Add fried fish and cook for 5 more minutes until flavors combine.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander if using.

Serve With

  • Steamed rice
