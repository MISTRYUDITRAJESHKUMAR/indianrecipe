Ingredients

  • 3 cups water
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp ghee or oil

Instructions

  1. Rinse and soak the moong dal for 30 minutes. Drain.
  2. In a pan or pressure cooker, cook dal with turmeric and salt until soft. Mash lightly.
  3. Heat ghee or oil in a separate pan. Add cumin seeds, dry red chilies, and asafoetida.
  4. Add grated ginger and saut√© briefly.
  5. Pour the tempering over the cooked dal and mix well.
  6. Simmer for 5 minutes to blend flavors. Adjust salt and consistency with water.
  7. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve hot.

Serve With

  • Steamed rice
  • Roti or paratha
  • A side of pickles or fried vegetables
