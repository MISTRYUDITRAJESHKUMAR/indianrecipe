Ingredients

  • 1 cup chhena (fresh paneer)
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • Oil for deep frying
  • 1 tsp rose water (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, knead the chhena until smooth. Add flour, baking soda, and cardamom powder, and knead again to form a smooth dough.
  2. Divide the dough into small balls, making them smooth and round.
  3. Heat oil in a deep pan over medium heat for frying.
  4. Fry the dough balls until they turn golden brown and puff up.
  5. Meanwhile, prepare sugar syrup by boiling water and sugar in a pan until the sugar dissolves completely. Add rose water to the syrup if desired.
  6. Once the pantuas are fried, drop them into the warm sugar syrup and let them soak for a few minutes.
  7. Remove from syrup and serve them warm or chilled.

Serve With

  • Enjoy as a delicious Bengali dessert!
