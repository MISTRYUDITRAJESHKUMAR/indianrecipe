Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice (any variety, preferably short grain or basmati)
  • 2 cups water
  • 1/2 tsp salt (optional)

Instructions

  1. Rinse the rice in water 2‚Äì3 times until the water runs clear.
  2. In a saucepan, add rice, water, and salt.
  3. Bring it to a boil on medium heat.
  4. Once boiling, reduce heat to low, cover, and let it simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. Turn off the heat and let it rest (covered) for 5 more minutes.
  6. Fluff with a fork and serve hot.
