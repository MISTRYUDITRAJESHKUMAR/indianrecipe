Ingredients

  • 1 liter full-fat milk
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice or vinegar
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp rose water (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat the milk in a heavy-bottomed pan. Once it comes to a boil, reduce the heat to low.
  2. Slowly add lemon juice or vinegar to the milk and stir. The milk will curdle and separate into whey and chhena (paneer).
  3. Once the curdling is complete, strain the mixture using a cheesecloth or muslin cloth to separate the chhena from the whey. Rinse the chhena under cold water to remove the lemon/vinegar taste.
  4. Press out the excess water from the chhena and knead it into a smooth dough.
  5. Divide the dough into small equal-sized balls and set them aside.
  6. In a large pan, bring water and sugar to a boil. Stir until the sugar dissolves completely to make the sugar syrup.
  7. Gently drop the prepared chhena balls into the boiling sugar syrup and cook for 15‚Äì20 minutes. The rasgullas will expand as they cook.
  8. Once they have cooked, remove the pan from the heat and let the rasgullas soak in the syrup for at least 1 hour before serving.

Serve With

  • Chilled, served in its syrup.
