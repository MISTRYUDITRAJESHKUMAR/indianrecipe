Ingredients

  • 1 liter full-fat milk
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice or vinegar
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
  • Chopped pistachios or almonds (for garnishing)
  • A few strands of saffron (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat the milk in a heavy-bottomed pan. Once it comes to a boil, reduce the heat to low.
  2. Slowly add lemon juice or vinegar to the milk, stirring continuously. The milk will curdle and separate into whey and chhena (paneer).
  3. Once the curdling is complete, strain the mixture using a cheesecloth or muslin cloth to separate the chhena from the whey. Rinse the chhena under cold water to remove the lemon/vinegar taste.
  4. Press out the excess water from the chhena and knead it into a smooth dough.
  5. Add powdered sugar and cardamom powder to the chhena. Knead well to combine the ingredients.
  6. Shape the mixture into small round or oval discs using your hands or molds.
  7. Garnish the Sandesh with chopped pistachios, almonds, or saffron strands (optional).
  8. Refrigerate the Sandesh for at least 1 hour before serving.

Serve With

  • Chilled, as a dessert or snack.
