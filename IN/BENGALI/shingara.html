Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 tbsp oil or ghee
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • Water (as needed)
  • 2 medium potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1/2 cup green peas
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, salt, and oil. Add water gradually to form a stiff dough. Rest for 20 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and ginger.
  3. Add mashed potatoes, peas, turmeric, salt, and garam masala. Mix well and cook for 5 minutes. Let it cool.
  4. Divide dough into balls. Roll each into a circle and cut in half. Form a cone and fill with the potato mixture.
  5. Seal the edges with water.
  6. Heat oil and deep fry the shingaras until golden brown and crisp.

Serve With

  • Tamarind chutney
  • Green chutney
  • Hot tea
