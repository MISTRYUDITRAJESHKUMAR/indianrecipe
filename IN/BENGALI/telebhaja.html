Ingredients

  • 1 large potato, sliced thinly
  • 1 medium-sized eggplant, sliced thinly
  • 1 small raw banana, sliced thinly
  • 1 cup chickpea flour (besan)
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp carom seeds (ajwain)
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, add chickpea flour, turmeric powder, red chili powder, cumin seeds, carom seeds, and salt.
  2. Gradually add water and make a smooth, thick batter.
  3. Dip the slices of potato, eggplant, and raw banana into the batter, coating them evenly.
  4. Heat oil in a frying pan or kadhai over medium heat.
  5. Fry the coated slices until golden brown and crispy on both sides.
  6. Remove them from the oil and drain excess oil on paper towels.

Serve With

  • Green chutney
  • Tamarind chutney
  • Hot tea
