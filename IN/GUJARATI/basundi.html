Ingredients

  • 1 liter full-fat milk
  • 1/4 cup sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 4â€“5 saffron strands (soaked in 1 tbsp warm milk)
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
  • 2 tbsp chopped almonds and pistachios
  • Optional: a few drops of rose water or kewra essence

Instructions

  1. Boil the milk in a heavy-bottomed pan, then simmer on low heat.
  2. Keep stirring and scraping the sides to prevent burning.
  3. Reduce the milk to almost half, then add sugar and mix well.
  4. Add saffron milk, cardamom powder, and chopped nuts.
  5. Simmer for 5â€“10 more minutes, then turn off the heat.
  6. Chill in the fridge before serving. Garnish with nuts before serving.

Serve With

  • Poori or lightly sweetened puran poli
  • As a dessert after a festive meal
