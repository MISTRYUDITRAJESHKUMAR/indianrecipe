Ingredients

  • 6-8 big green chilies (mild variety)
  • 1/2 cup roasted peanut powder
  • 2 tbsp sesame seeds
  • 2 tbsp grated coconut (optional)
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp oil (for cooking)
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/4 tsp asafoetida (hing)

Instructions

  1. Slit the green chilies lengthwise and remove seeds if desired.
  2. In a bowl, mix peanut powder, sesame seeds, coconut, lemon juice, sugar, and salt to make the stuffing.
  3. Stuff each chili with the mixture carefully.
  4. Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  5. Add asafoetida and place the stuffed chilies in the pan.
  6. Cover and cook on low flame, turning occasionally, until chilies are soft and slightly charred.

Serve With

  • Gujarati Thali
  • Khichdi
  • Thepla or Roti
