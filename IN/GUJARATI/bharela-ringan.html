üçÜ Bharela Ringan (Stuffed Eggplant) Recipe

Ingredients

  • 6-8 small brinjals (eggplants), slit in a cross pattern
  • 1/2 cup groundnut powder (roasted and crushed peanuts)
  • 1/4 cup grated coconut
  • 2 tbsp sesame seeds
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh coriander
  • 2 tsp ginger-green chili paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp cumin-coriander powder
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp jaggery (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • 3-4 tbsp oil for cooking

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix groundnut powder, coconut, sesame seeds, coriander, spices, lemon juice, and salt to prepare stuffing.
  2. Stuff this mixture into the slit brinjals carefully.
  3. Heat oil in a deep pan, place the stuffed brinjals gently, and sprinkle remaining stuffing if any.
  4. Cover and cook on low flame, stirring occasionally, until brinjals are tender and fully cooked.
  5. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot.

Serve With

  • Rotli (roti) or bajra/bhakri
  • Gujarati dal and rice
üí° Tip: Add a few drops of water while cooking to prevent burning and help even cooking.
