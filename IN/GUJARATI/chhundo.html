Ingredients

  • 2 cups grated raw mango (firm, sour)
  • 1.5 cups sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds (optional)
  • 1/4 tsp asafoetida (hing)

Instructions

  1. Peel and grate raw mango. Mix with salt and turmeric. Let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Add sugar to the grated mango and stir until it starts to dissolve.
  3. Keep the mixture in sunlight (covered with a muslin cloth) for 4â€“5 days, stirring daily.
  4. Once the sugar forms a thick syrup and the mango softens, mix in chili powder, cumin, and asafoetida.
  5. Store in a clean, dry glass jar. Keep refrigerated or in a cool place.

Serve With

  • Thepla or paratha
  • Puri and khichdi
  • As a side with traditional Gujarati meals
