Instructions

  1. Cook the tuvar dal with turmeric and water in a pressure cooker until soft.
  2. For the dhokli dough, mix whole wheat flour, turmeric, red chili powder, salt, and oil. Add water little by little to form a smooth dough.
  3. Roll the dough into small balls, then flatten them into discs (about 2-inch diameter) and set aside.
  4. Once the dal is cooked, add jaggery, green chilies, and ginger paste. Bring to a boil.
  5. Slowly drop the dhoklis into the simmering dal. Cook for 10‚Äì12 minutes until the dhoklis are soft and cooked through.
  6. In a separate pan, heat ghee or oil and add cumin seeds, mustard seeds, hing, curry leaves, and dried red chilies. Once they splutter, pour the tempering over the dal dhokli.
  7. Let it simmer for another 5 minutes, then serve hot, garnished with fresh coriander.
