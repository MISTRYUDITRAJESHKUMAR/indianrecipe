Ingredients

  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1 tbsp semolina (rava/sooji)
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp ginger-green chili paste
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tsp fruit salt (Eno)
  • 3/4 cup water (adjust as needed)

For Tempering:

  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp sesame seeds
  • 1â€“2 green chilies, slit
  • 10â€“12 curry leaves
  • 2 tbsp water

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix gram flour, semolina, lemon juice, sugar, salt, and ginger-chili paste. Add water and mix into a smooth batter.
  2. Just before steaming, add fruit salt and mix gently. Pour immediately into a greased steaming tray.
  3. Steam for 15â€“20 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  4. Allow it to cool slightly, then cut into square pieces.
  5. Heat oil in a small pan, add mustard seeds, sesame seeds, curry leaves, and chilies. Let them splutter.
  6. Add water and pour the tempering over the cut dhoklas.

Serve With

  • Green chutney
  • Sweet tamarind chutney
  • Tea or buttermilk
