Ingredients

  • 2 cups gram flour (besan)
  • 1/4 tsp carom seeds (ajwain)
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper powder
  • Pinch of baking soda
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed to form soft dough
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine gram flour, carom seeds, turmeric, pepper, baking soda, and salt.
  2. Add oil and mix thoroughly. Gradually add water to make a soft dough.
  3. Use a sev or ganthiya maker to press dough into hot oil.
  4. Fry on medium heat until slightly golden but still soft (avoid over-frying to retain softness).
  5. Remove and drain on absorbent paper.

Serve With

  • Masala tea
  • Raw papaya chutney
  • Green chutney
