Ingredients

  • 1 cup mawa (khoya)
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg powder
  • 1/4 cup chopped dry fruits (cashews, almonds, pistachios)
  • 1 cup refined flour (maida)
  • 2 tbsp ghee (for dough)
  • Ghee for deep frying
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar (for coating)

Instructions

  1. Prepare dough with maida, ghee, and water. Set aside.
  2. In a pan, roast mawa until aromatic and slightly golden. Let it cool.
  3. Add sugar, cardamom, nutmeg, and dry fruits to the cooled mawa. Mix well.
  4. Divide mawa mixture into small portions and shape them round.
  5. Roll small circles from dough, place a mawa ball inside, and seal it carefully.
  6. Deep fry on low-medium heat until golden brown.
  7. Let them cool, then coat with powdered sugar evenly.

Serve With

  • Warm milk or saffron milk
  • As a festive sweet during Chandani Padva or Diwali
