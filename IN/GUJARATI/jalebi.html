Ingredients

For Jalebi Batter:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 2 tbsp cornflour
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • Water as needed
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric (for color)

For Sugar Syrup:

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • Few saffron strands
  • 1/2 tsp lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Mix all batter ingredients and rest for 6‚Äì8 hours or overnight.
  2. Prepare sugar syrup by boiling sugar and water until sticky (not stringy). Add lemon juice, saffron, and cardamom.
  3. Heat oil or ghee in a wide pan. Pour batter into a squeeze bottle or cloth cone.
  4. Pipe spirals of batter into hot oil and fry until golden and crispy.
  5. Immediately dip fried jalebis in warm syrup for a few seconds. Remove and serve hot.

Serve With

  • Fafda (traditional pairing)
  • Rabri or milk
  • As a festive sweet
