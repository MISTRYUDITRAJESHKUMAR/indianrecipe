Ingredients

  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1 cup sour yogurt
  • 2 cups water
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp ginger-green chili paste
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp oil (for greasing and tempering)

For Tempering:

  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp sesame seeds
  • 8‚Äì10 curry leaves
  • 2 green chilies, chopped
  • A pinch of asafoetida (hing)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix besan, yogurt, water, turmeric, salt, and ginger-chili paste into a smooth batter.
  2. Cook the mixture in a non-stick pan on medium heat, stirring continuously to avoid lumps, until it thickens.
  3. Quickly spread a thin layer of the hot batter on greased plates or kitchen counter using a spatula.
  4. Once cool, cut into strips and gently roll them up.
  5. Heat oil in a small pan, add mustard seeds, sesame seeds, curry leaves, green chilies, and hing. Let it splutter.
  6. Pour the tempering over the rolled Khandvi and garnish with coriander and grated coconut (optional).

Serve With

  • Green coriander chutney
  • Sweet tamarind chutney
  • Masala tea
