Ingredients

  • 2 cups shredded cabbage
  • 2 medium potatoes, diced
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/4 tsp asafoetida (hing)
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander-cumin powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add mustard seeds. Once they splutter, add cumin seeds and asafoetida.
  2. Add turmeric and diced potatoes. Cook for 5-6 minutes on medium heat.
  3. Add shredded cabbage and mix well.
  4. Sprinkle red chili powder, coriander-cumin powder, and salt. Mix thoroughly.
  5. Cover and cook for 10â€“12 minutes, stirring occasionally, until vegetables are tender.
  6. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot.

Serve With

  • Roti or Phulka
  • Dal and Rice
  • Gujarati Kadhi
