Ingredients

  • 10â€“12 garlic cloves
  • 2 tbsp red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice or 1 small piece tamarind (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil

Instructions

  1. Peel the garlic cloves.
  2. In a mixer grinder, add garlic, red chili powder, cumin, salt, and lemon juice/tamarind if using.
  3. Grind to a coarse or smooth paste as per your preference.
  4. Heat oil in a small pan and mix it into the chutney for enhanced flavor and shelf life.
  5. Store in an airtight container and refrigerate.

Serve With

  • Thepla, bhakri, or roti
  • Khichdi or rice dishes
  • As a side in a traditional Gujarati thali
