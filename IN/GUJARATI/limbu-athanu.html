Ingredients

  • 15 medium-sized lemons
  • 1/2 cup salt (non-iodized)
  • 1/2 cup sugar (optional for sweet version)
  • 1 tbsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tbsp red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 tsp asafoetida (hing)
  • 1 tbsp mustard seeds (crushed)
  • 1/2 cup mustard oil (optional)

Instructions

  2. In a clean bowl, mix lemon pieces with salt and turmeric. Store in a glass jar for 7 days. Stir daily with a dry spoon.
  3. After 7 days, add red chili powder, hing, mustard seeds, and sugar (if making sweet pickle). Mix well.
  4. Heat mustard oil until smoking and let it cool. Pour over pickle (optional for more flavor).
  5. Keep covered in sunlight for 3‚Äì5 days. Stir daily.
  6. The pickle is ready once lemons soften and soak flavors.
