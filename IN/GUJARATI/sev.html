üçú Sev Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups gram flour (besan)
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/4 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp carom seeds (ajwain)
  • 2 tbsp oil (hot, for moyan)
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as required to make dough
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix besan, turmeric, chili powder, carom seeds, and salt.
  2. Add hot oil (moyan) and mix with your fingers.
  3. Gradually add water to make a smooth and soft dough.
  4. Fill the dough into a sev maker fitted with a fine-holed disc.
  5. Heat oil and directly press sev into the oil in circular motion.
  6. Fry on medium heat until crisp and golden. Remove and drain on paper.

Serve With

  • Tea or coffee
  • As topping on chaat or poha
  • Mixed with murmura or namkeen
üí° Tip: Ensure the oil is not too hot, or the sev may burn quickly.
