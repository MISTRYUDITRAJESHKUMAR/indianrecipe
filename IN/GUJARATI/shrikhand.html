Ingredients

  • 2 cups hung curd (yogurt strained overnight)
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • A few strands of saffron soaked in 1 tbsp warm milk
  • 2 tbsp chopped pistachios and almonds
  • Optional: rose water or kewra essence (a few drops)

Instructions

  1. Strain yogurt overnight in a muslin cloth to get thick hung curd.
  2. In a bowl, whisk the hung curd until smooth and creamy.
  3. Add powdered sugar and mix well.
  4. Add cardamom powder and saffron milk. Mix until fully blended.
  5. Chill in the fridge for 1‚Äì2 hours for best flavor.
  6. Garnish with chopped nuts and serve chilled.

Serve With

  • Puri
  • As a dessert after a traditional Gujarati meal
