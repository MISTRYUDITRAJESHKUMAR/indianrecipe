Ingredients

  • 1 cup whole wheat flour (gehun ka atta)
  • 1/2 cup ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1/2 cup grated jaggery (gur)
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • Chopped dry fruits for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat ghee in a pan and add wheat flour.
  2. Roast on low flame, stirring constantly, until the mixture turns golden brown and aromatic.
  3. Turn off the heat and let it cool slightly (just warm, not hot).
  4. Add grated jaggery and mix well until it melts and combines with the flour.
  5. Add cardamom powder and mix thoroughly.
  6. Transfer to a greased plate and flatten evenly.
  7. Garnish with chopped dry fruits if desired. Let it set and cut into squares.

Serve With

  • Tea or milk as an energizing snack
  • Festive meals or traditional thali
üí° Tip: Use fresh jaggery for a soft texture and better flavor. Do not overcook after adding jaggery.
