Ingredients

  • 2 ridge gourds (turiya), peeled and chopped
  • 1 cup patra slices (steamed colocasia leaf rolls)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/4 tsp asafoetida (hing)
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander-cumin powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 tsp sugar (optional)
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add mustard seeds. When they splutter, add cumin seeds and asafoetida.
  2. Add turmeric and chopped turiya. Cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Add patra slices and mix gently.
  4. Sprinkle red chili powder, coriander-cumin powder, sugar, and salt. Mix well.
  5. Cover and cook for 10‚Äì15 minutes until turiya is soft and flavors are well blended.
  6. Garnish with chopped coriander and serve hot.

Serve With

  • Bhakri or Thepla
  • Plain Rice and Dal
  • Gujarati Kadhi
üí° Tip: Use fresh patra for best texture and flavor.
