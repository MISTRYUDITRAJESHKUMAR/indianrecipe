Ingredients

  • 1 cup surti papdi (flat green beans), split and stringed
  • 1 cup valor papdi (broad beans), split and stringed
  • 1 cup purple yam, peeled and cubed
  • 1 cup sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1/2 cup green peas
  • 1/2 cup ripe banana pieces
  • 1/2 cup methi muthia (fenugreek dumplings)
  • 1/2 cup fresh grated coconut
  • 2 tbsp chopped coriander leaves
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds
  • 2 tsp ginger-green chili paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 2 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp jaggery
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for cooking

Instructions

  1. Prepare all vegetables and muthias. Mix coconut, coriander, sesame seeds, ginger-chili paste, jaggery, lemon juice, and dry spices in a bowl.
  2. Heat oil in a large pan or pressure cooker. Add the mixed masala paste and sautÃ© for a few minutes.
  3. Add all vegetables one by one and gently mix with the masala.
  4. Add salt and little water. Cover and cook on low flame (or pressure cook for 2 whistles).
  5. Add methi muthia and banana pieces in the last 5 minutes. Simmer until all vegetables are tender and flavors are absorbed.
  6. Garnish with fresh coriander and coconut before serving.

Serve With

  • Poori or roti
  • Gujarati kadhi and steamed rice
