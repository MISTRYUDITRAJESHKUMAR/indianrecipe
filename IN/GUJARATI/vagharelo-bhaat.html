Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably a day old)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/4 tsp asafoetida (hing)
  • 1-2 green chilies (slit)
  • 5-6 curry leaves
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 tsp sugar (optional)
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add mustard seeds. Let them splutter.
  2. Add cumin seeds, asafoetida, green chilies, and curry leaves. Saut√© for a few seconds.
  3. Add turmeric powder and mix well.
  4. Add cooked rice, salt, and sugar. Mix gently to coat the rice evenly with the tempering.
  5. Cook for 3‚Äì5 minutes until the rice is heated through.
  6. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot.

Serve With

  • Yogurt or raita
  • Fried papad
  • Mango or lime pickle
