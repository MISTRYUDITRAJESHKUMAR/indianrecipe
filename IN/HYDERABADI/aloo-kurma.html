Ingredients

  • 3 medium-sized potatoes, peeled and chopped
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/4 cup yogurt, whisked
  • 1/4 cup coconut milk (optional, for creaminess)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 cup water (adjust for consistency)
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  2. Add chopped onions and sautÃ© until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and sautÃ© for a minute, then add chopped tomatoes. Cook until tomatoes soften and oil separates from the masala.
  4. Add turmeric, red chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Stir well and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Add the chopped potatoes and mix well. Add water and cover to cook until potatoes are tender (around 10-12 minutes).
  6. Once potatoes are cooked, add whisked yogurt and coconut milk (if using), and cook for 5 minutes to blend the flavors.
  7. Sprinkle garam masala and stir. Simmer for another 2-3 minutes, then remove from heat.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot.

Serve With

  • Steamed rice
  • Chapati or paratha
  • Onion salad or raita
