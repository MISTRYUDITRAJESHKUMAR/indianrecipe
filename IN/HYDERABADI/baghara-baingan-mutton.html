Instructions

  1. Dry roast coconut, peanuts, sesame, and poppy seeds. Cool and grind to a paste with a little water.
  2. Heat oil and fry brinjals (slit but intact) until 80% cooked. Set aside.
  3. In the same pan, add more oil if needed. Fry onions till golden brown. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook till raw smell disappears.
  4. Add mutton pieces, turmeric, red chili, coriander powder, and salt. Saut√© until mutton changes color.

  7. Add the fried brinjals, simmer till they are fully cooked and absorb the flavors.
  8. Garnish with coriander and serve hot.
