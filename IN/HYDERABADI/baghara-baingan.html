Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp oil (for shallow frying)
  • 2 tbsp peanuts
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds
  • 1 tbsp grated dry coconut
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder

Instructions

  1. Dry roast peanuts, sesame seeds, and coconut. Let them cool, then grind into a paste with little water.
  2. Shallow fry the slit brinjals in oil until soft and slightly browned. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, add a bit of oil. Saut√© mustard seeds, cumin seeds, curry leaves, and onions until golden.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and saut√© for a minute. Add turmeric, chili powder, and salt.
  5. Mix in the ground paste and cook for a few minutes until oil separates.
  6. Add tamarind pulp and some water to adjust consistency. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  7. Add the fried brinjals back, cover, and cook until they absorb the flavors.
  8. Serve hot with steamed rice or biryani.

Serve With

  • Steamed basmati rice
  • Hyderabadi biryani
  • Chapati or roti
