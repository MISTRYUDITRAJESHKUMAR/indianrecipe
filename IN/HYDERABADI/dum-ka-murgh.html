Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup fried onions
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • 3 tbsp ghee or oil

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, red chili powder, coriander powder, salt, and fried onions. Let it rest for at least 2 hours or overnight.
  2. Heat ghee/oil in a heavy-bottom pan. Add marinated chicken and cook for 5 minutes on high flame.
  3. Lower the heat, cover with a tight-fitting lid and cook on low flame (dum) for 40‚Äì45 minutes or until chicken is tender.
  4. Add cream and garam masala. Mix gently and cook for another 5 minutes.
  5. Garnish with chopped coriander and mint leaves. Serve hot.

Serve With

  • Roti or Naan
  • Jeera Rice
  • Onion Raita
