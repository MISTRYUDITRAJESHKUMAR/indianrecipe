Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup falooda sev (vermicelli)
  • 1/4 cup basil seeds (sabja seeds)
  • 1/2 cup rose syrup
  • 2 cups cold milk
  • 2 tbsp sugar (or to taste)
  • 1 scoop vanilla ice cream
  • Crushed ice (optional)
  • Chopped nuts (almonds, pistachios) for garnish
  • Cherries or rose petals for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Soak the basil seeds in water for about 15 minutes. Once they expand, drain the water and set them aside.
  2. Boil the falooda sev in water for 3-4 minutes, then drain and rinse them under cold water. Set aside.
  3. In a large glass, add a tablespoon of rose syrup at the bottom.
  4. Add a spoonful of soaked basil seeds and a layer of cooked falooda sev on top of the syrup.
  5. Pour in the cold milk and add sugar. Stir well to combine.
  6. Add some crushed ice if you prefer a colder drink.
  7. Top the drink with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.
  8. Garnish with chopped nuts, and optionally, a cherry or rose petals for an extra touch.
  9. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Serve With

  • Fruit salad
  • Chilled snacks like samosas or pakoras
