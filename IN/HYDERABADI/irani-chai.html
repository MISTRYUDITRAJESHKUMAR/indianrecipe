Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup full cream milk
  • 2 tsp strong black tea leaves (preferably loose leaf tea)
  • 2 tbsp sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1-2 crushed cardamom pods (optional, for flavor)
  • 1/4 tsp saffron strands (optional, for richness)

Instructions

  1. In a saucepan, add 2 cups of water and bring it to a boil.
  2. Add the loose tea leaves and cardamom pods to the boiling water. Let it simmer for about 5-7 minutes, allowing the tea to brew strongly.
  3. Add the milk to the tea mixture and bring it back to a boil. Reduce the heat and let it simmer for another 5 minutes, allowing the milk to blend with the tea.
  4. Stir in the sugar and saffron (if using) and let it dissolve completely.
  5. Once the tea is ready, strain it into cups or glasses. Serve hot with snacks like biscuits or samosas.

Serve With

  • Osmania biscuits
  • Samosas
  • Mirchi Bajji
