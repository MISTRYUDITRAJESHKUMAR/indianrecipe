Ingredients

  • 250g minced mutton or chicken (keema)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp chopped coriander leaves
  • Samosa pastry sheets or homemade dough
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Heat a pan and cook the keema with all spices, onion, and ginger-garlic paste until the moisture dries out completely.
  2. Add chopped coriander leaves and allow the mixture to cool.
  3. Fill the cooled keema into samosa sheets or rolled dough, fold into triangles, and seal edges using water or flour paste.
  4. Deep fry the samosas in hot oil until golden brown and crispy.
  5. Serve hot with chutney or ketchup.

Serve With

  • Tamarind or mint chutney
  • Masala chai or evening tea
  • Onion rings with lemon
