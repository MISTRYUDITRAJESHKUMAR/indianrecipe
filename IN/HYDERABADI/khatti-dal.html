Ingredients

  • 1 cup yellow lentils (toor dal)
  • 1 tbsp tamarind pulp
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 2-3 dried red chilies
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp ghee or oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • 1/2 tsp hing (asafoetida) - optional

Instructions

  1. Wash the lentils thoroughly and pressure cook them with turmeric powder and enough water until soft (about 3-4 whistles).
  2. Heat ghee or oil in a pan and add cumin seeds, mustard seeds, dried red chilies, and hing. Let them splutter.
  3. Add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown. Then, add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft, then add red chili powder and salt.
  5. Add tamarind pulp and a little water (adjust to desired consistency) and let it simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. Once the dal is cooked, add it to the tempering mixture and stir well. Let it simmer for another 5-7 minutes to blend the flavors.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot.

Serve With

  • Steamed rice
  • Chapati or paratha
  • Onion salad or papad
üí° Tip: Adjust the tanginess by adding more or less tamarind according to your taste preference.
