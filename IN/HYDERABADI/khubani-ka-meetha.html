Ingredients

  • 200g dried apricots (khubani)
  • 1/2 cup sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • 1 tbsp ghee
  • 1/4 cup slivered almonds (for garnish)
  • 1/4 cup pistachios (for garnish)
  • 1/4 cup fresh cream (optional, for richness)

Instructions

  1. Soak the dried apricots in warm water for 2-3 hours or overnight until they become soft.
  2. Drain the apricots and reserve the soaking water. Cut the apricots into halves or quarters, removing the seeds.
  3. In a heavy-bottomed pan, heat the ghee and add the chopped apricots. Cook for a few minutes until they soften further.
  4. Add the reserved soaking water to the pan along with sugar and cardamom powder. Stir and let it cook on medium heat for 10-12 minutes, until the apricots are well-cooked and syrupy.
  5. If desired, add fresh cream to the dish for a rich, creamy texture. Stir well to combine.
  6. Garnish with slivered almonds and pistachios.
  7. Serve warm or chilled. It can be enjoyed as a dessert or a sweet accompaniment with meals.

Serve With

  • Vanilla ice cream
  • Phirni
  • Layered pudding
üí° Tip: You can adjust the amount of sugar depending on the sweetness of the apricots, as dried apricots can vary in sweetness.
