Ingredients

  • 500g boneless mutton slices (thin and tender)
  • 2 tbsp raw papaya paste (for tenderizing)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee

Instructions

  1. Marinate the mutton slices with papaya paste, ginger-garlic paste, spices, lemon juice, and salt for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  2. Heat a thick iron tawa (griddle) on medium heat until very hot.
  3. Apply a little oil or ghee and place marinated meat slices on the hot surface.
  4. Cook by pressing the meat with a spatula or stone for even grilling.
  5. Flip and cook both sides until well browned and cooked through.
  6. Serve hot with onion rings and lemon wedges.

Serve With

  • Roomali Roti
  • Mint Chutney
  • Onion Salad
