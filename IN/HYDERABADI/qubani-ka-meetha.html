Ingredients

  • 1 cup dried apricots (Qubani)
  • 1/4 cup sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 cup water (or as needed)
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
  • 1/4 cup blanched almonds, slivered
  • 1/4 cup fresh cream or thick yogurt (optional)
  • Rose water or kewra water (optional, for fragrance)

Instructions

  1. Wash the dried apricots thoroughly. Soak them in warm water for 2-3 hours or overnight to soften them.
  2. Drain the apricots and remove the pits. Set the apricots aside.
  3. In a saucepan, add the soaked apricots, sugar, and water. Cook on medium heat, stirring occasionally, until the apricots soften and the syrup thickens (about 15-20 minutes).
  4. Add cardamom powder and stir well. Cook for another 5 minutes to blend the flavors.
  5. If you prefer a smoother texture, mash the apricots lightly with a spoon or blend them slightly.
  6. Transfer the Qubani ka Meetha into serving bowls and garnish with slivered almonds and a drizzle of fresh cream or yogurt.
  7. For an added fragrance, you can add a few drops of rose water or kewra water before serving.
  8. Serve chilled or at room temperature.

Serve With

  • Ice cream
  • Fresh cream
  • Sweet lassi or a refreshing drink
üí° Tip: If you like a more concentrated syrup, cook it longer until the liquid reduces. You can also add a little saffron for extra flavor.
