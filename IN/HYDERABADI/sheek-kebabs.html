Ingredients

  • 500g minced mutton or chicken (finely ground)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 finely chopped onion
  • 2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves, finely chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • Juice of 1/2 lemon
  • 1 tbsp gram flour (optional, for binding)
  • Skewers and oil for grilling or pan-frying

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients thoroughly in a bowl. Let the mixture rest for 30 minutes in the refrigerator.
  2. Shape the meat around skewers into cylindrical kebabs.
  3. Grill on a barbecue or cook on a hot skillet with a little oil, turning until evenly browned and cooked through.
  4. Serve hot with naan, chutney, and sliced onions.

Serve With

  • Butter naan or rumali roti
  • Mint-coriander chutney
  • Pickled onions and lemon wedges
üí° Tip: Use metal skewers or soaked bamboo skewers to prevent sticking and ensure even cooking.
