Ingredients

  • 500g minced mutton or chicken
  • 1/2 cup soaked chana dal (split Bengal gram)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2-3 green chilies
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 small onion (finely chopped)
  • 1/4 cup chopped mint and coriander leaves
  • 2 tbsp hung curd or thick yogurt
  • Oil for shallow frying

Instructions

  1. Cook minced meat with soaked dal, spices, and green chilies until soft and water evaporates.
  2. Cool and grind into a smooth paste.
  3. Mix in onion, herbs, and yogurt to form the kebab mixture.
  4. Shape into flat patties and refrigerate for 15â€“20 minutes.
  5. Shallow fry on a non-stick pan until golden brown on both sides.
  6. Serve hot with mint chutney and onion rings.

Serve With

  • Paratha or naan
  • Green chutney and onion salad
  • Lemon wedges
