Ingredients

  • 500g mutton pieces (boneless or with bone)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp yogurt (optional)
  • Oil for shallow frying

Instructions

  1. Marinate mutton with all spices, lemon juice, and yogurt. Let it rest for at least 1 hour.
  2. Pressure cook or simmer the mutton until tender. Ensure all liquid is absorbed.
  3. Heat oil in a pan, shallow fry the cooked mutton until crispy and browned on all sides.
  4. Serve hot with onion rings, lemon wedges, and naan or rice.

Serve With

  • Hyderabadi Biryani
  • Rumali roti or naan
  • Onion salad and mint chutney
üí° Tip: Use minimal oil and a non-stick pan for healthier frying.
