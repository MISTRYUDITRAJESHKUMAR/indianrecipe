Ingredients

  • 4 large tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 green chili, slit
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 cup water (adjust to desired consistency)
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • 1 tbsp jaggery (optional, for sweetness)

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds, mustard seeds. Let them splutter.
  2. Add chopped onions and saut√© until they turn golden brown. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes, green chili, turmeric, red chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Cook for 5-7 minutes until the tomatoes soften and release their juice.
  4. Add water to adjust the consistency, then cook the mixture for another 5 minutes on low heat.
  5. For a sweet touch, add jaggery and cook for another minute.
  6. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot.

Serve With

  • Chapati or paratha
  • Steamed rice
  • Raita
üí° Tip: You can adjust the sweetness by increasing or decreasing the amount of jaggery based on your preference.
