Ingredients

  • 3-4 medium potatoes (boiled & sliced or chopped)
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced (optional)
  • 2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves, chopped

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  2. Add cumin seeds, hing, and green chilies. SautÃ© for a few seconds.
  3. Add sliced onions (if using) and cook until translucent.
  4. Add turmeric and red chili powder. Mix well.
  5. Add the boiled potatoes and salt. Mix everything gently without mashing the potatoes.
  6. Cook for 3-5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until flavors blend.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot.

Serve With

  • Poori or chapati
  • Batata Vada stuffing
  • Pithla-Bhakri or plain rice
