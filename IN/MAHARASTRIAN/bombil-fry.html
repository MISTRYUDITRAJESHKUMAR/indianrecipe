üêü Bombil Fry (Bombay Duck Fry)

Ingredients

  • 6-8 Bombil (Bombay Duck) fish, cleaned and de-shelled
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/4 cup rice flour
  • 2 tbsp semolina (rava)
  • Oil for shallow frying

Instructions

  1. Clean and wash the bombil fish thoroughly. Pat them dry using a kitchen towel.
  2. In a bowl, mix ginger-garlic paste, turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala, lemon juice, and salt to make a marinade.
  3. Coat the bombil fish with the marinade and let it rest for at least 30 minutes to absorb the flavors.
  4. In a shallow plate, mix rice flour and semolina. Coat the marinated fish with this dry mixture, making sure it's evenly covered.
  5. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat and shallow fry the fish until golden and crispy on both sides (around 3-4 minutes per side).
  6. Once fried, remove the fish from the pan and place it on a paper towel to absorb excess oil.
  7. Serve hot with a squeeze of lemon juice and enjoy your crispy bombil fry!

Serve With

  • Steamed Rice
  • Onion salad
  • Green chutney
üí° Tip: Be sure to fry the bombil on medium heat to ensure it cooks evenly without burning.
