Ingredients

  • 500g chicken pieces (with bone)
  • 2 large onions, finely sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large pot and add cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  2. Add the onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  3. Stir in ginger-garlic paste and saut√© for a minute.
  4. Add the tomatoes, turmeric, red chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Cook until the oil separates from the masala.
  5. Add the chicken pieces and cook for 5-7 minutes, turning occasionally.
  6. Pour in 2 cups of water and bring it to a boil. Reduce the heat and simmer for 20-25 minutes until the chicken is tender and the gravy thickens.
  7. Sprinkle garam masala and cook for another 5 minutes.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot.

Serve With

  • Chappati or Paratha
  • Plain rice or jeera rice
  • Fried papad
