Ingredients

  • 2 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup besan (gram flour)
  • 2 tbsp rice flour (for crispiness)
  • 2-3 green chilies, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp carom seeds (ajwain)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves, chopped
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix sliced onions with salt. Let it sit for 10 minutes to release moisture.
  2. Add besan, rice flour, green chilies, turmeric, chili powder, ajwain, and coriander to the onions.
  3. Mix everything using your hands. Avoid adding water; the onion moisture should be enough.
  4. Heat oil in a deep pan. Drop small portions of the mixture into the hot oil.
  5. Fry on medium heat until golden and crisp. Remove on paper towels.
  6. Serve hot with chutney or ketchup and a cup of masala chai.

Serve With

  • Green chutney or tamarind chutney
  • Masala chai or cutting tea
  • Dry garlic chutney (optional)
