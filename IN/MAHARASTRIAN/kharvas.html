Ingredients

  • 1 liter full-fat milk
  • 1/2 cup fresh colostrum milk (or regular milk if not available)
  • 1/4 cup sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
  • 1/4 tsp saffron strands (optional)
  • 1 tbsp cornflour (optional, to thicken)
  • Chopped pistachios or almonds for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a heavy-bottomed pan, heat the full-fat milk and bring it to a boil. Then, reduce the heat and let it simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  2. Add the colostrum milk (if available) to the simmering milk and mix well.
  3. Stir in sugar, cardamom powder, and saffron strands (if using). Continue to cook for another 5 minutes until the sugar dissolves completely.
  4. If you'd like a thicker consistency, dissolve cornflour in a little cold milk and add this mixture to the boiling milk. Stir well until it thickens slightly.
  5. Remove the pan from heat and allow the milk to cool to room temperature.
  6. Pour the cooled milk mixture into small containers or bowls for steaming.
  7. Steam the containers in a steamer or pressure cooker (without the pressure) for 25-30 minutes until the kharvas is set and firm to the touch.
  8. Let the kharvas cool before serving. Garnish with chopped pistachios or almonds.
  9. Serve chilled and enjoy!

Serve With

  • Fresh Fruits
  • Sweet Porridge or Vermicelli
