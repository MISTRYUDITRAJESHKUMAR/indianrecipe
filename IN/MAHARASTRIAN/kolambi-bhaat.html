Ingredients

  • 1 cup basmati rice (washed and soaked)
  • 250g cleaned prawns (kolambi)
  • 1 large onion, finely sliced
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 2 tbsp grated coconut (fresh or dry)
  • 2-3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, minced
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala or goda masala
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish
  • 2 cups water

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a deep pan. Saut√© onions until golden brown.
  2. Add garlic, ginger, green chilies, and saut√© for a minute.
  3. Add tomatoes and cook until soft.
  4. Add turmeric, red chili powder, and garam masala. Mix well.
  5. Add grated coconut and saut√© for a couple of minutes.
  6. Add prawns and cook for 2‚Äì3 minutes until they turn pink.
  7. Add soaked rice and mix gently with the masala.
  8. Pour in water, add salt, and bring to a boil. Cover and cook on low flame until rice is done.
  9. Let it rest for 5 minutes, fluff gently with a fork, and garnish with coriander.

Serve With

  • Sol kadhi
  • Koshimbir (Maharashtrian salad)
