Ingredients

  • 1 cup sprouted moth beans (matki)
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 1 chopped tomato
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 2-3 cups water
  • Chopped coriander leaves for garnish
  • Farsan or sev (for topping)
  • 4 pav buns

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds and cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  2. Add chopped onions and saut√© until golden. Add ginger-garlic paste and saut√© for a minute.
  3. Add tomatoes and cook until soft. Add turmeric, chili powder, and garam masala.
  4. Now add the sprouted moth beans and mix well.
  5. Add salt and water. Cover and simmer for 15‚Äì20 minutes or until the beans are cooked and the curry thickens slightly.
  6. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
  7. Serve hot in a bowl, topped with farsan/sev. Serve with butter-toasted pav on the side.

Serve With

  • Butter-toasted pav
  • Onion slices
  • Wedges of lemon
