Ingredients

  • 2 cups flattened rice (poha)
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 8-10 curry leaves
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 small potato, chopped (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp sugar (optional)
  • 2 tbsp chopped coriander leaves
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp peanuts

Instructions

  1. Rinse poha in a colander under running water for a few seconds. Let it sit and drain completely. Fluff with a fork and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add peanuts and fry until crisp. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same oil, add mustard seeds and let them splutter. Add curry leaves, green chili, and chopped onions. SautÃ© until onions turn translucent.
  4. Add chopped potatoes if using and cook until soft.
  5. Add turmeric powder and salt. Mix well.
  6. Add the rinsed poha, sugar, and mix gently on low flame.
  7. Cook for 2â€“3 minutes. Turn off the heat and add lemon juice and fried peanuts.
  8. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot.

Serve With

  • Hot chai
  • Green chutney
  • Sev on top (optional)
