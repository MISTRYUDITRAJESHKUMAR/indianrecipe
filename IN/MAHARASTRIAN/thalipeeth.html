Ingredients

  • 1 cup multigrain flour (or bhajani)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1â€“2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp fresh coriander, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Water, as needed
  • Oil or ghee for roasting

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine all ingredients except oil. Add water gradually to form a soft dough.
  2. Divide dough into small balls. Flatten each on a plastic sheet or banana leaf using wet hands.
  3. Make a hole in the center of each thalipeeth to help it cook evenly.
  4. Heat a tawa, drizzle oil, and place the thalipeeth on it. Cook on medium flame until golden and crisp on both sides.
  5. Repeat with remaining dough. Serve hot.

Serve With

  • Fresh curd or yogurt
  • Thecha (green chili-garlic chutney)
  • White butter or pickle
