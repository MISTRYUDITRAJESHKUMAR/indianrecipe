Ingredients

  • 4 pav buns
  • 2 large potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 8‚Äì10 curry leaves
  • 2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Chopped coriander (optional)
  • Oil for deep frying
  • For batter: 1 cup besan (gram flour), 1/4 tsp turmeric, a pinch of baking soda, salt, and water

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds and let them crackle. Add curry leaves, green chilies, and ginger-garlic paste.
  2. Add turmeric, mashed potatoes, salt, and coriander. Mix well. Let the mixture cool.
  3. Make small balls of the potato mixture.
  4. In a bowl, prepare a thick batter using besan, turmeric, salt, soda, and water.
  5. Heat oil for deep frying. Dip each ball into the batter and fry until golden brown.
  6. Slice pav buns, apply chutney if available, and place a vada inside.
  7. Serve hot with fried green chilies and dry garlic chutney.

Serve With

  • Dry garlic chutney
  • Fried green chilies
  • Masala chai
