Instructions for Zunka

  1. Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter. Add hing, garlic, and green chilies.
  2. Add onions and sautÃ© until golden. Then add turmeric and red chili powder.
  3. Lower the flame, add besan gradually while stirring to avoid lumps.
  4. Sprinkle little water and cook, stirring continuously, until the mixture thickens and becomes dry.
  5. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves. Zunka is ready.

Instructions for Bhakri

  1. In a bowl, mix flour and salt. Gradually add warm water and knead into a soft dough.
  2. Take a small ball of dough, flatten and pat into a round using your palms or a rolling pin.
  3. Cook on a hot tawa on both sides until puffed and lightly browned. Optionally, roast directly on flame for puffiness.
  4. Serve hot with a dollop of ghee or butter.

Serve With

  • Raw onion and green chili
  • Thecha or garlic chutney
  • Fresh curd or buttermilk
