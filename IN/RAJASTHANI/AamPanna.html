Ingredients

  • 2 raw mangoes (medium size)
  • 1/2 cup sugar or jaggery (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 tsp black salt
  • 1/2 tsp roasted cumin powder
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper powder
  • Fresh mint leaves (optional)
  • 2-3 cups chilled water
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Instructions

  1. Boil or roast mangoes until soft. Let them cool and peel the skin.
  2. Scoop out the pulp and blend it with sugar/jaggery, salt, cumin, pepper, and mint (if using).
  3. Add chilled water and blend again until smooth.
  4. Serve cold with ice cubes. Garnish with mint or cumin powder.
  5. Store concentrate in fridge for up to 5 days. Dilute with water before serving.

Serve With

  • Spicy snacks
  • As a summer cooler
  • Alongside Indian meals
