Instructions

  1. Peel garlic cloves. If using dry red chilies, soak them in warm water for 15 minutes.
  2. In a small pan, heat oil and fry cumin seeds and garlic for 2‚Äì3 minutes.

  5. Store in airtight container. Refrigerate for up to a week.
