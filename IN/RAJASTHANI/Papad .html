Ingredients

  • 1 cup urad dal flour (or moong dal flour)
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper (coarsely crushed)
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/4 tsp asafoetida (hing)
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • Oil for greasing

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix urad dal flour, crushed pepper, cumin, hing, and salt.
  2. Gradually add water and knead into a stiff dough.
  3. Divide into small balls and roll them thin on a greased surface.
  4. Sun dry on a clean cloth or tray for 1‚Äì2 days until crisp and dry.
  5. Once dried, store in airtight containers.
  6. Roast or fry papads before serving.

Serve With

  • Rajasthani thali
  • Dal Baati Churma
  • Curry or khichdi
  • As a crunchy snack
