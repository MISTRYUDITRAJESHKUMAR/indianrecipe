Ingredients

  • 2 cups raw mango pieces (or lemon, chili, carrot, etc.)
  • 2 tbsp mustard seeds (split)
  • 2 tbsp fennel seeds (saunf)
  • 1 tsp fenugreek seeds (methi)
  • 1 tbsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 cup mustard oil (or sesame oil)

Instructions

  1. Wash and dry the mango pieces completely. Moisture can spoil the pickle.
  2. Mix all spices and salt with the mango pieces.
  3. Heat mustard oil until smoky, then let it cool slightly.
  4. Pour over the mango mixture and mix well.
  5. Transfer to a clean, dry jar. Seal and let it sit in sunlight for 4â€“5 days, stirring daily.
  6. Once matured, store in a cool dry place. Use as desired.

Serve With

  • Parathas and Theplas
  • Dal-Chawal
  • Khichdi
  • Puri or Roti
