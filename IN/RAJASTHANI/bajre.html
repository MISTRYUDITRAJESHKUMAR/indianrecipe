Ingredients

  • 1 cup bajra (pearl millet) flour
  • Warm water (as needed)
  • Salt to taste
  • Ghee or butter for serving

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix bajra flour with a pinch of salt.
  2. Add warm water gradually and knead to form a soft dough.
  3. Divide into balls. Flatten using palms or rolling pin between plastic sheets.
  4. Cook on a hot tawa (griddle) on both sides until golden spots appear.
  5. Apply ghee or butter and serve hot.

Serve With

  • Garlic chutney or green chutney
  • Sarson ka saag or Rajasthani kadhi
