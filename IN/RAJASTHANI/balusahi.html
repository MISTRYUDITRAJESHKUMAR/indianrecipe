Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1/4 cup ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 cup curd (yogurt)
  • 1/2 cup cold water (as needed)
  • Ghee or oil for frying
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup water (for syrup)
  • Cardamom powder and saffron (optional)
  • Chopped pistachios for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, baking soda, and ghee using your fingertips until crumbly.
  2. Add curd and cold water gradually to make a stiff dough. Rest for 30 mins.
  3. Make small balls and slightly flatten them, pressing gently in the center.
  4. Heat ghee/oil on low heat and fry the balushahi slowly until golden brown.
  5. In a pan, prepare one-string sugar syrup with sugar and water. Add cardamom.
  6. Dip fried balushahi in warm syrup for a few minutes, then remove and cool.
  7. Garnish with pistachios or silver leaf before serving.

Serve With

  • Tea during festivals
  • Traditional Rajasthani thali
