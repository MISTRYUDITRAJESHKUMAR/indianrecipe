Ingredients

  • 1 cup besan (gram flour)
  • 1/4 cup moong dal flour (optional, for authentic texture)
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper (crushed)
  • 1/4 tsp asafoetida (hing)
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp carom seeds (ajwain)
  • Salt to taste
  • Water (to knead dough)
  • Oil (for deep frying)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix besan, moong dal flour, spices, and salt.
  2. Add water gradually and knead into a soft, non-sticky dough.
  3. Heat oil in a kadhai on medium flame.
  4. Fill dough in a sev maker (fine hole plate) and press directly into hot oil.
  5. Fry until crisp and golden. Remove and drain on paper towel.
  6. Cool completely before storing in an airtight container.

Serve With

  • Masala chai
  • As a snack or topping for chaats
