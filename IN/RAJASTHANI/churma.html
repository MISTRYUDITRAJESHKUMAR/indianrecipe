Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1/2 cup ghee (clarified butter)
  • 3/4 cup powdered jaggery or sugar
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
  • 2 tbsp grated dry coconut (optional)
  • 2 tbsp chopped dry fruits (almonds, cashews, raisins)
  • Ghee for frying

Instructions

  1. Make stiff dough using whole wheat flour and ghee. Roll into thick muthis (fist shapes).
  2. Deep fry on low heat until golden and crisp. Cool and crush into coarse powder.

  4. Mix in jaggery or sugar, cardamom, coconut, and dry fruits.
  5. While warm, shape into laddus. Press tightly for shape to hold.
