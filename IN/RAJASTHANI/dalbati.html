For Baati:
  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • ¬º cup semolina (sooji)
  • ¬º cup ghee
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
For Churma:
  • 2 cooked baatis (crushed)
  • 2 tbsp powdered sugar

  • ¬Ω tsp cardamom powder

Instructions

  1. To make Dal: Boil all dals with turmeric and salt. Temper with ghee, cumin, onion, tomato, ginger garlic, chili powder.
  2. To make Baati: Mix flour, sooji, salt, ghee. Add water and knead. Shape into balls and bake or roast until golden brown.
  3. To make Churma: Crush baatis, mix with ghee, sugar, and cardamom powder.

Serve With

  • Dal poured over crushed baatis
  • Ghee generously on top
  • Churma as a sweet side
