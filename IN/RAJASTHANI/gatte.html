Ingredients

For Gatte (Gram Flour Dumplings):
  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1 tsp carom seeds (ajwain)
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 2 tbsp curd
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
For Gravy:
  • 1 cup yogurt (curd)
  • 1 tbsp gram flour (besan)
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • Pinch of asafoetida (hing)
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed

Instructions

  1. Mix all gatte ingredients, form a dough, divide into rolls and boil in water until firm. Cut into pieces.
  2. In a bowl, mix yogurt, gram flour, and all spices for the gravy. Whisk well.
  3. Heat oil, add mustard, cumin, hing. Add yogurt mixture and stir continuously until it simmers.
