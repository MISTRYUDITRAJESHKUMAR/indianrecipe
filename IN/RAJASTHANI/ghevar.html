Ingredients

  • 1 cup refined flour (maida)
  • 1/4 cup ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1/2 cup cold milk
  • 1/2 cup chilled water (as needed)
  • 1/4 tsp lemon juice
  • Ghee or oil for deep frying
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup water (for syrup)
  • Cardamom powder, saffron strands, chopped nuts (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, beat ghee and milk together until creamy. Add lemon juice.
  2. Add flour gradually and mix, adding chilled water until you get a thin, smooth batter.
  3. Heat ghee in a tall, wide pan. Pour batter from a height using a ladle, in the center, to form a honeycomb pattern.
  4. Fry on medium flame until golden and crisp. Drain and keep aside.
  5. Make sugar syrup to 1-string consistency. Dip ghevar briefly in syrup.
  6. Garnish with saffron strands, cardamom, and chopped dry fruits. Optionally, top with rabri.

Serve With

  • Rabri (sweet thickened milk)
  • As a festive dessert
