Ingredients

  • 1 cup urad dal (soaked 4‚Äì5 hours)
  • 1/4 tsp orange food color (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
  • Ghee or oil for frying
  • 1.5 cups sugar
  • 3/4 cup water
  • A few saffron strands (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Grind soaked urad dal into a thick, fluffy batter without adding much water.
  2. Add orange food color and whisk well to aerate.
  3. Make sugar syrup: boil sugar and water until one-string consistency. Add cardamom and lemon juice.
  4. Fill batter into a piping bag or muslin cloth with small hole.
  5. Heat ghee in a wide flat pan. Pipe imarti shapes and fry on medium flame till golden and crisp.
  6. Soak immediately in warm sugar syrup for 2‚Äì3 minutes. Remove and serve warm or at room temperature.

  • Festive meals
  • Hot milk or rabri
