Ingredients

  • 1 cup sour curd
  • 2 tbsp gram flour (besan)
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/4 tsp asafoetida (hing)
  • 2 dry red chilies
  • 1 tbsp ghee
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 cups water

Instructions

  1. Whisk curd with besan, turmeric, red chili powder, salt, and water. Ensure no lumps.
  2. Heat ghee in a pan. Add mustard seeds, cumin, hing, and dry red chilies.
  3. Once they splutter, pour in the curd mixture and stir continuously.
  4. Simmer on low heat for 15‚Äì20 minutes, stirring occasionally until kadhi thickens.
  5. Adjust salt and serve hot.

Serve With

  • Steamed rice or bajra roti
  • Green chutney and salad
