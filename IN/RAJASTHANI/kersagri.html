Ingredients

  • 1 cup dried sangri (desert beans)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • ¬Ω tsp cumin seeds
  • Pinch of asafoetida (hing)
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • ¬Ω tsp dry mango powder (amchur)
  • 1 tsp fennel seeds (saunf)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp yogurt (optional)

Instructions

  1. Soak ker and sangri overnight. Rinse and boil until tender.
  2. Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds, cumin, fennel, and hing.
  3. Add boiled ker and sangri. Mix in turmeric, chili, coriander powder, and salt.
  4. Add yogurt if using and cook until oil separates and flavors blend.

Serve With

  • Bajra roti or missi roti
  • Chopped onions and curd
