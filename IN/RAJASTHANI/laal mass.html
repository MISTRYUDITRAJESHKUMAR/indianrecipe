Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp mustard oil
  • 2 medium onions (sliced)
  • 1 cup curd (yogurt)
  • 10-12 dry red chilies (soaked & ground)
  • 1 tsp garlic paste
  • 1 tsp ginger paste
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 2-3 cloves
  • 1 small cinnamon stick
  • 2 green cardamoms
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish (optional)
