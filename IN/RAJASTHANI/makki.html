Ingredients

  • 1 cup makki ka atta (cornmeal)
  • Warm water (as needed)
  • Salt to taste
  • Ghee or butter for serving

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix cornmeal with a pinch of salt.
  2. Gradually add warm water and knead into a soft dough.
  3. Take a portion of dough and pat into a round shape using your palms or plastic sheets.
  4. Cook on a hot tawa until both sides are golden with brown spots.
  5. Apply ghee or butter and serve hot.

Serve With

  • Sarson ka Saag
  • White butter and jaggery
