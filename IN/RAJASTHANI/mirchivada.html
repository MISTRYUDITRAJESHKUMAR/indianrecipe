Ingredients

For Filling:

  • 6 large green chilies (mild variety like Bhavnagri or banana peppers)
  • 2 medium potatoes (boiled & mashed)
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp amchur powder (dry mango powder)
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste

For Batter:

  • 1 cup besan (gram flour)
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric
  • 1/4 tsp ajwain (carom seeds)
  • Salt to taste
  • Water (as needed)
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions

  1. Slit the chilies lengthwise and remove seeds carefully.
  2. Mix mashed potatoes with spices for the filling.
  3. Stuff the potato filling into the slit green chilies.
  4. Prepare a smooth batter with besan, turmeric, ajwain, salt, and water.
  5. Dip stuffed chilies into batter and coat well.
  6. Heat oil and deep-fry until golden and crisp.
  7. Serve hot with chutney or ketchup.

Serve With

  • Green chutney
  • Tamarind chutney
  • Masala chai
