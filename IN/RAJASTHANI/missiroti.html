Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup whole wheat flour
  • 1/2 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1/2 tsp ajwain (carom seeds)
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 green chili, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp finely chopped coriander
  • Salt to taste
  • Water to knead
  • Ghee or oil for roasting

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients in a bowl and knead into a soft dough using water.
  2. Divide the dough into small balls and roll each into a roti.
  3. Cook on a hot tawa, applying ghee or oil on both sides, until golden brown.
  4. Serve hot with pickle, curd, or sabzi.

Serve With

  • Curd or raita
  • Rajasthani kadhi or sabzi
