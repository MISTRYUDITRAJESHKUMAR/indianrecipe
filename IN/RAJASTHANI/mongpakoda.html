Ingredients

  • 1 cup yellow moong dal (soaked 3â€“4 hours)
  • 1 green chili, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp ginger (grated)
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/4 tsp hing (asafoetida)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Drain soaked moong dal and grind coarsely without adding water.
  2. Add chopped onion, green chili, ginger, and all spices. Mix well.
  3. Heat oil in a kadhai on medium flame.
  4. Drop small spoonfuls of batter into hot oil.
  5. Fry till golden brown and crispy. Remove on paper towel.

Serve With

  • Green chutney
  • Imli chutney
  • Masala chai
