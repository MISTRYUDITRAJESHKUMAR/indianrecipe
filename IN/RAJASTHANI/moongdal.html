Ingredients

  • 1 cup yellow moong dal (soaked 4‚Äì5 hours)
  • 1 cup ghee
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • Chopped almonds, cashews, pistachios (for garnish)
  • Saffron strands (optional)

Instructions

  1. Grind soaked moong dal to a coarse paste without water.
  2. Heat ghee in a pan and add the paste. Stir continuously on medium flame.
  3. Cook until the dal turns golden brown and releases aroma (15‚Äì20 mins).
  4. In a separate pan, heat milk, water, and sugar until sugar dissolves.
  5. Add this syrup to the roasted dal carefully (it may splutter).
  6. Mix well and cook until the ghee separates and halwa thickens.
  7. Add cardamom powder and garnish with dry fruits.

Serve With

  • Hot during winters
  • As a dessert in Rajasthani thali
