Ingredients

For Dough:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1/4 cup ghee or oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Water to knead

For Filling:

  • 2 cups finely chopped onions
  • 2 tbsp besan (gram flour)
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp fennel seeds
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp amchur (dry mango powder)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, ghee, and salt. Add water and knead a soft dough. Rest 30 mins.
  2. Heat oil, add cumin & fennel seeds. Add onions and sautÃ© until soft.
  3. Add besan and spices. Cook until mixture is dry. Cool completely.
  4. Divide dough and filling. Flatten dough, stuff filling, and seal properly.
  5. Deep fry on medium-low heat until golden and crispy.

Serve With

  • Green chutney
  • Tamarind chutney
  • Chai (Tea)
