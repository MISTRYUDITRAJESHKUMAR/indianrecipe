Ingredients

  • 1 cup whole wheat flour
  • 2 tbsp curd (yogurt)
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • 1/8 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • Water to knead
  • Butter or ghee for brushing

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, salt, baking powder, baking soda, and curd.
  2. Knead into a soft dough using water. Let it rest for 30 minutes.
  3. Divide into balls and roll into medium-thick rotis.
  4. Cook on a hot tawa or in a tandoor until golden spots appear.
  5. Brush with butter or ghee before serving.

Serve With

  • Paneer Butter Masala
  • Dal Makhani
  • Chicken Curry
