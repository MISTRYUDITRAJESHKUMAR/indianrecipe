Ingredients

  • 1 cup green moong dal (whole)
  • 2-3 green chilies
  • 1 inch ginger
  • 2 tbsp rice (optional, for crispness)
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 small onion (finely chopped, optional)
  • Cumin seeds (optional)
  • Oil or ghee for roasting

Instructions

  1. Soak moong dal (and rice if using) for 4-6 hours or overnight. Drain.
  2. Grind soaked dal with green chilies, ginger, and salt to a smooth batter (add water as needed).
  3. Heat a non-stick pan or tawa, pour a ladle of batter and spread like a dosa.
  4. Drizzle oil/ghee around edges. Optionally, sprinkle chopped onions and cumin.
  5. Cook till golden and crisp. Flip if desired.
  6. Serve hot with ginger chutney or coconut chutney.

Serve With

  • Ginger chutney
  • Coconut chutney
  • Sambar
