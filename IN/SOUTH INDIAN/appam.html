Ingredients

  • 1 cup raw rice
  • 1/4 cup cooked rice
  • 1/2 cup grated coconut
  • 1/4 tsp dry yeast
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed

Instructions

  1. Soak raw rice for 4-5 hours. Drain.
  2. Grind soaked rice with cooked rice, coconut, sugar, and water to a smooth batter.
  3. Dissolve yeast in warm water and add to the batter. Mix well.
  4. Ferment the batter overnight or for 8-10 hours.
  5. Add salt, mix gently. Heat an appam pan, pour batter and swirl to coat edges.
  6. Cover and cook for 2-3 minutes until edges are crisp and center is soft.
  7. Serve hot with vegetable stew or coconut milk.

Serve With

  • Vegetable stew
  • Coconut milk
  • Chutney
üìù Tip: Use fresh coconut for best flavor, and ferment in a warm place for soft, fluffy appams.
