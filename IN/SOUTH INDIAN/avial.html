Ingredients

  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, raw banana, yam, etc.), cut into strips
  • 1/2 cup grated coconut
  • 2 green chilies
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp coconut oil
  • 8-10 curry leaves

Instructions

  1. Cook the vegetables with turmeric and salt until just tender.
  2. Grind coconut, green chilies, and cumin to a coarse paste.
  3. Add the paste to the cooked vegetables and simmer for a few minutes.
  4. Remove from heat, then add whisked yogurt and mix well.
  5. Drizzle coconut oil and add curry leaves on top.
  6. Serve warm with rice.

Serve With

  • Steamed rice
  • Adai
  • South Indian thali
