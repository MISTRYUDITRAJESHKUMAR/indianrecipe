Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup toor dal (pigeon peas)
  • 1/2 cup rice
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, peas)
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tbsp tamarind pulp
  • 2 tbsp Bisi Bele Bath powder
  • 2 tbsp ghee or oil
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 8-10 curry leaves
  • 2 dry red chilies
  • Cashews (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook toor dal and rice separately until soft.
  2. Boil vegetables until tender and set aside.
  3. In a pan, heat ghee, add mustard seeds, red chilies, curry leaves, and saut√© onions and tomatoes.
  4. Add vegetables, turmeric, tamarind pulp, salt, and Bisi Bele Bath powder. Mix well.
  5. Add cooked dal and rice. Mix thoroughly and add water to adjust consistency.
  6. Simmer for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  7. Garnish with fried cashews if desired. Serve hot.

Serve With

  • Potato chips or boondi
  • Raita or curd
  • Pickle
üç≤ Tip: Use homemade or store-bought Bisi Bele Bath powder for authentic flavor. Adjust tamarind for tanginess.
