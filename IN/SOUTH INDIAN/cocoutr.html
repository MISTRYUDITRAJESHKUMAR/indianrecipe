Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 1 cup grated fresh coconut
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp urad dal
  • 1 tsp chana dal
  • 2 dried red chilies
  • 10 curry leaves
  • 2 green chilies, chopped
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/4 tsp hing (asafoetida)
  • 2 tbsp roasted cashews (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  2. Add urad dal, chana dal, red chilies, curry leaves, and green chilies.
  3. Fry till dals turn golden. Add hing and grated coconut. SautÃ© for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Turn off the heat. Mix in the cooked rice and salt.
  5. Garnish with roasted cashews. Serve warm.

Serve With

  • Pickle
  • Papad
  • Raita
