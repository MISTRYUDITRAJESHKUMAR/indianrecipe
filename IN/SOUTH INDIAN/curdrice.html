Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (cooled)
  • 1.5 cups thick curd (yogurt)
  • 1/4 cup milk (optional for creaminess)
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp urad dal
  • 2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1 dried red chili
  • 10 curry leaves
  • 1 tbsp chopped coriander leaves
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 tbsp grated carrot or pomegranate (for garnish, optional)

Instructions

  1. Mash the cooled rice slightly and mix with curd and milk. Add salt.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds. Let them splutter.
  3. Add urad dal, green chilies, red chili, and curry leaves. SautÃ© briefly.
  4. Pour the tempering into the curd-rice mixture and mix well.
  5. Garnish with coriander, grated carrot, or pomegranate.
  6. Serve chilled or at room temperature.

Serve With

  • Pickles
  • Papad
