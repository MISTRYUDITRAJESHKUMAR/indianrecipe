Ingredients

  • 1 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 3 cups rice (regular or parboiled)
  • 1/2 tsp fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Water (as needed for soaking and grinding)
  • Oil or ghee for cooking

Instructions

  1. Soak rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds separately for 6 hours or overnight.
  2. Grind to a smooth batter with water, then mix both batters together.
  3. Ferment the batter overnight or 8â€“12 hours until it doubles in volume.
  4. Add salt and mix gently.
  5. Heat a non-stick tawa or dosa pan, pour a ladle of batter, and spread it thin.
  6. Drizzle oil or ghee around the edges. Cook until golden and crisp.
  7. Serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar.

Serve With

  • Coconut chutney
  • Tomato chutney
  • Sambar
  • Potato masala (for masala dosa)
