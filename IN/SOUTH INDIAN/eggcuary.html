Ingredients

  • 6 boiled eggs, peeled
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan, saut√© onions until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook until raw smell disappears.
  3. Add tomato puree, turmeric, red chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Cook until oil separates.
  4. Make small slits on boiled eggs and add them to the gravy.
  5. Cook for 5-7 minutes on low heat.
  6. Sprinkle garam masala and garnish with coriander leaves. Serve hot.

Serve With

  • Steamed Rice
  • Roti or Paratha
