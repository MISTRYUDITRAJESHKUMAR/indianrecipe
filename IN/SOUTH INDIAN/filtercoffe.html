Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp ground coffee (South Indian filter coffee blend)
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • Sugar to taste
  • South Indian coffee filter

Instructions

  1. Place ground coffee in the top compartment of the filter and press gently with the plunger.
  2. Pour hot water over the coffee powder and close the lid.
  3. Let it drip into the bottom compartment for 10-15 minutes.
  4. Boil milk and add sugar to taste.
  5. Mix one part decoction with one part hot milk or adjust strength as desired.
  6. Pour back and forth between two tumblers to create froth and serve hot.

Serve With

  • South Indian Breakfast (Idli, Dosa, Upma)
  • Biscuits or Savories
  • On its own as a refreshing beverage
