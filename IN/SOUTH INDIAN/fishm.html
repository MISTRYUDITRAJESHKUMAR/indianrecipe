Ingredients

  • 500 g fish (kingfish or seer fish), cleaned and cut
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 2 tbsp coconut oil
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper powder
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat coconut oil in a pan, add sliced onions, green chilies, and curry leaves. Saut√© until onions are translucent.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and saut√© until raw smell disappears.
  3. Add turmeric powder and black pepper powder. Mix well.
  4. Add fish pieces and salt, cook gently for 5-7 minutes.
  5. Pour in coconut milk and simmer on low heat until fish is cooked and the gravy thickens slightly.
  6. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot.

Serve With

  • Steamed Rice
  • Appam or Idiyappam
