Ingredients

  • 2 cups idli rice (or parboiled rice)
  • 1 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1/2 tsp fenugreek seeds (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as required
  • Oil for greasing idli moulds

Instructions

  1. Soak rice and dal (with fenugreek) separately for 6 hours or overnight.
  2. Grind urad dal to a smooth, fluffy batter. Grind rice to a slightly coarse texture.
  3. Mix both batters, add water as needed, and allow to ferment 8‚Äì12 hours.
  4. Once risen, add salt and mix gently.
  5. Grease idli moulds, pour batter in each mould, and steam for 10‚Äì15 minutes.
  6. Check with a toothpick; if it comes out clean, idlis are ready.
  7. Serve hot with chutney and sambar.

Serve With

  • Coconut chutney
  • Tomato chutney
  • Sambar
  • Milagai podi (idli podi)
