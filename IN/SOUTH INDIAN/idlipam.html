Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice flour (fine variety)
  • 1.25 cups water
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp oil
  • Fresh grated coconut (optional, for topping)

Instructions

  1. Boil water with salt and oil in a saucepan.
  2. Add rice flour and mix quickly to form a smooth dough.
  3. Cover and let it rest for 5-10 minutes.
  4. Knead warm dough until smooth. Fill into an idiappam press.
  5. Press into greased idli plates or steamer tray in spiral form.
  6. Steam for 8-10 minutes on medium heat.
  7. Serve hot with coconut milk, vegetable stew, or curry.

Serve With

  • Coconut milk sweetened with jaggery
  • Vegetable stew
  • Kerala-style egg curry or kadala curry
