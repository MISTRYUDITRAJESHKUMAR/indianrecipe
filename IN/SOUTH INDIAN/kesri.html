Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup rava (semolina)
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1.5 cups water
  • 1/4 cup ghee
  • Few saffron strands or a pinch of food color
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • 10 cashews
  • 8 raisins

Instructions

  1. Heat 1 tbsp ghee in a pan, fry cashews and raisins, then set aside.
  2. Roast the rava until aromatic. Do not brown.
  3. Boil water with saffron strands or food color.
  4. Slowly add the roasted rava to the boiling water while stirring.
  5. Cook until the mixture thickens.
  6. Add sugar and stir continuously until it dissolves.
  7. Add ghee gradually and mix well until the mixture leaves the pan.
  8. Stir in cardamom powder, cashews, and raisins.

Serve With

  • Upma (for chow chow bath combo)
  • As a dessert with South Indian meals
  • Festive meals
