Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp urad dal
  • 1 tsp chana dal
  • 2 dried red chilies
  • 1-2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 10-12 curry leaves
  • Juice of 1 large lemon
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp roasted peanuts
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  2. Add urad dal, chana dal, dried red chilies, green chilies, and curry leaves. Saut√© until dals turn golden brown.
  3. Add turmeric powder and mix well.
  4. Add cooked rice and salt, mix gently to combine all ingredients well.
  5. Turn off heat and add lemon juice. Mix well.
  6. Garnish with roasted peanuts and fresh coriander leaves. Serve warm.

Serve With

  • Yogurt
  • Pickle
