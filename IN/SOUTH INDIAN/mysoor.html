Ingredients

  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1 cup ghee
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup water

Instructions

  1. Grease a tray or plate and set aside.
  2. In a pan, mix sugar and water. Boil to make one-string syrup.
  3. Add sifted gram flour gradually, stirring continuously to avoid lumps.
  4. Start adding hot ghee in batches while stirring.
  5. Keep stirring until the mixture starts leaving the sides of the pan and turns porous.
  6. Quickly pour into the greased tray and spread evenly.
  7. Let it cool slightly, then cut into squares.

Serve With

  • South Indian festive meals
  • As a sweet snack
  • Special occasions like Diwali
