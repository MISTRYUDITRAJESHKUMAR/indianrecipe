Ingredients

  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • Oil for greasing the pan

Instructions

  1. Grind the soaked rice with water to a very fine batter.
  2. Add salt and more water to make it very thin and runny.
  3. Heat a non-stick or cast iron pan. Grease lightly.
  4. Pour a ladle of batter, swirl the pan to spread it thinly.
  5. Cover and cook for a minute. No need to flip.
  6. Fold and serve hot.

Serve With

  • Coconut Chutney
  • Vegetable Kurma
  • Chicken Curry
