Ingredients

  • 1 cup jaggery (grated or powdered)
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 tsp roasted cumin powder
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper powder
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Few mint leaves (optional)
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder

Instructions

  1. Dissolve jaggery in warm water and stir well.
  2. Add roasted cumin powder, black pepper powder, and cardamom powder.
  3. Mix in lemon juice and mint leaves.
  4. Chill in the refrigerator or add ice cubes before serving.
  5. Serve cold as a refreshing traditional drink.

Serve With

  • During festivals like Ram Navami
  • As a refreshing summer drink
  • With light snacks or sweets
