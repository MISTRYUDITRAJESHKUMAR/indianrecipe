Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • Water (as needed to make dough)
  • Additional oil/ghee for cooking

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, sugar, salt, and oil. Add water gradually and knead into a soft dough.
  2. Cover and rest the dough for at least 1 hour.
  3. Divide into equal balls. Roll each ball thinly, apply oil, and roll like a spiral.
  4. Flatten gently and roll again into a disc.
  5. Cook on a hot tawa with oil or ghee until golden spots appear.
  6. Fluff the parotta by clapping or gently crushing between hands to reveal layers.

Serve With

  • Chettinad Chicken
  • Kurma
  • Salna or Gravy
