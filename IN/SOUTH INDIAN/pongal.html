üçö Pongal Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup rice
  • 1/4 cup yellow moong dal
  • 1 tsp black pepper (whole or crushed)
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 inch ginger (finely chopped)
  • A few curry leaves
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • 10-12 cashews
  • Salt to taste
  • 4 cups water

Instructions

  1. Dry roast moong dal till aromatic. Rinse dal and rice well.
  2. Pressure cook rice and dal together with water and salt for 4-5 whistles.
  3. Heat ghee in a pan. Fry cashews till golden, then add cumin, pepper, ginger, and curry leaves.
  4. Pour the tempering over the cooked rice-dal mixture and mix well.
  5. Serve hot with coconut chutney or sambar.

Serve With

  • Coconut chutney
  • Sambar
  • Pickle
üçõ Tip: Adjust ghee to enhance the flavor and richness of Pongal.
