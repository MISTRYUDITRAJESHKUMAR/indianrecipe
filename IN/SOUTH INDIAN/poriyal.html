Ingredients

  • 2 cups chopped vegetables (like cabbage, beans, or carrots)
  • 1/2 cup grated coconut
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp urad dal
  • 2 dried red chilies
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp oil

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  2. Add urad dal, red chilies, curry leaves, and green chili.
  3. Add chopped vegetables and salt. Sprinkle a little water.
  4. Cover and cook until vegetables are tender.
  5. Mix in the grated coconut and sautÃ© for 2-3 minutes.
  6. Serve hot as a side dish.

Serve With

  • Rice and Sambar
  • Rasam
  • South Indian Thali
