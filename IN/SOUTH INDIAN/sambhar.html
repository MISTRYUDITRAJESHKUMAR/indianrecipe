Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup toor dal (pigeon peas)
  • 1.5 cups mixed vegetables (carrot, drumstick, pumpkin, etc.)
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tbsp tamarind pulp
  • 2 tbsp sambar powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tsp oil or ghee
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/4 tsp asafoetida (hing)
  • 8-10 curry leaves
  • 2 dry red chilies

Instructions

  1. Pressure cook toor dal with turmeric until soft and mash it well.
  2. Boil the vegetables with a little salt until cooked.
  3. In a pot, combine mashed dal, cooked vegetables, tamarind pulp, and sambar powder.
  4. Add water to adjust consistency and bring to a boil. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. In a small pan, heat oil or ghee, add mustard seeds, red chilies, curry leaves, and asafoetida for tempering.
  6. Pour the tempering into the sambar and mix well.
  7. Serve hot with rice, idli, dosa, or vada.

Serve With

  • Steamed rice
  • Idli or dosa
  • Medu vada
