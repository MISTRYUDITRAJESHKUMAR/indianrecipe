üçõ Tamarind Rice (Puliyodarai) Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 1 lemon-sized ball of tamarind
  • 2 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp urad dal
  • 1 tsp chana dal
  • 2 dried red chilies
  • 10-12 curry leaves
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 2 tbsp roasted peanuts
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 tsp hing (asafoetida)

Instructions

  1. Soak tamarind in warm water for 15 minutes and extract thick pulp.
  2. Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add urad dal, chana dal, red chilies, curry leaves, and peanuts. Fry until golden.
  4. Add turmeric and hing, then pour in the tamarind extract.
  5. Cook the tamarind paste until it thickens and oil separates. Add salt to taste.
  6. Mix the paste into cooked rice and combine well. Rest for 15 minutes before serving.

Serve With

  • Papad
  • Curd or Buttermilk
üçã Tip: Tamarind paste can be made ahead and stored in the refrigerator for quick mixing.
