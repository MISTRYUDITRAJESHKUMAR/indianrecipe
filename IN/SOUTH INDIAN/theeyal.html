Ingredients

  • 1 cup chopped vegetables (like pearl onions, drumstick, or ridge gourd)
  • 1/2 cup grated coconut
  • 2 tsp coriander seeds
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 2 dried red chilies
  • 1/2 tsp fenugreek seeds
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 gooseberry-sized tamarind
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • Curry leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp coconut oil

Instructions

  1. Dry roast grated coconut until dark brown. Add coriander, cumin, red chilies, and fenugreek; roast and grind to a paste.
  2. Soak tamarind in warm water and extract juice.
  3. Cook vegetables with salt and turmeric until tender.
  4. Add tamarind extract and boil for 5 minutes.
  5. Add ground coconut paste and simmer until oil separates.
  6. Temper mustard seeds and curry leaves in coconut oil; add to theeyal.
  7. Serve hot with rice.

Serve With

  • Steamed Rice
  • Papad
  • Pickles
