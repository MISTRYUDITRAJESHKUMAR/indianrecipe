Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice flour
  • 1/2 cup jaggery (grated)
  • 1 ripe banana (mashed)
  • 2 tbsp coconut bits
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda (optional)
  • 1 tbsp ghee
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions

  1. Melt jaggery in a little water and strain to remove impurities.
  2. Mix rice flour, mashed banana, jaggery syrup, cardamom, coconut bits, and baking soda into a thick batter.
  3. Let the batter rest for at least 1 hour.
  4. Heat oil in an appam pan (appe pan), pour batter into each cavity.
  5. Fry until golden brown and crisp on both sides.
  6. Remove and drain excess oil using paper towels.

Serve With

  • As a tea-time snack
  • During Onam or other Kerala festivals
  • With coconut chutney (optional)
