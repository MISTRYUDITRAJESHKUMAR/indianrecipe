Ingredients

  • 1 cup semolina (rava/sooji)
  • 1 tbsp ghee or oil
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp urad dal
  • 1 tsp chana dal
  • 1-2 green chilies (chopped)
  • 1 medium onion (chopped)
  • A few curry leaves
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 2.5 cups water
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander and lemon (optional)

Instructions

  1. Dry roast the semolina until lightly golden and aromatic. Set aside.
  2. Heat oil or ghee in a pan. Add mustard seeds, urad dal, chana dal. Let them splutter.
  3. Add ginger, green chilies, curry leaves, and chopped onions. Saut√© till onions are translucent.
  4. Add water and salt. Bring to a boil.
  5. Lower the flame and slowly add the roasted semolina while stirring continuously to avoid lumps.
  6. Cover and cook for 2‚Äì3 minutes until water is absorbed and upma is fluffy.
  7. Garnish with coriander and serve hot with coconut chutney or pickle.

Serve With

  • Coconut chutney
  • Tomato chutney
  • Pickle
  • Hot tea or filter coffee
