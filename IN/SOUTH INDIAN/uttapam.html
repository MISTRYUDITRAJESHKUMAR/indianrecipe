Ingredients

  • 2 cups dosa/idli batter (fermented)
  • 1 small onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 tomato (finely chopped)
  • 1 green chili (finely chopped)
  • 1/4 cup capsicum (optional, finely chopped)
  • Chopped coriander leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil or ghee for cooking

Instructions

  1. Heat a tawa or non-stick pan and grease it lightly with oil or ghee.
  2. Pour a ladleful of dosa batter and spread it slightly to form a thick pancake.
  3. Sprinkle chopped onions, tomatoes, green chilies, capsicum, and coriander on top.
  4. Press lightly with a spatula so toppings stick to the batter.
  5. Drizzle oil or ghee around the edges.
  6. Cook on medium flame till the base is golden brown, then flip and cook the other side lightly.
  7. Serve hot with coconut chutney or sambar.

Serve With

  • Coconut chutney
  • Tomato chutney
  • Sambar
  • Mint chutney
