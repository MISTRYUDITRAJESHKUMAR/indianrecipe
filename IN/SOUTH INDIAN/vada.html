Ingredients

  • 1 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1-2 green chilies (finely chopped)
  • 1 tsp black pepper (crushed)
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • A few curry leaves (chopped)
  • 1 inch ginger (grated)
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions

  1. Soak urad dal for 4-5 hours or overnight.
  2. Drain and grind to a thick, smooth batter without adding too much water.
  3. Add salt, green chilies, pepper, cumin, ginger, and curry leaves to the batter.
  4. Heat oil in a deep pan. Wet your hands, take a portion of batter, shape like a donut, and gently drop into hot oil.
  5. Fry on medium heat till golden and crisp on both sides.
  6. Remove and drain excess oil using paper towels.
  7. Serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar.

Serve With

  • Coconut chutney
  • Sambar
  • Tomato chutney
  • Mint chutney
