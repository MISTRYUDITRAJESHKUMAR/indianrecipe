Ingredients

  • 2 cups gram flour (besan)
  • 1/2 tsp carom seeds (ajwain)
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper powder
  • Pinch of baking soda
  • 2 tbsp hot oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed to knead dough
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix gram flour, turmeric, carom seeds, black pepper, salt, and baking soda.
  2. Add hot oil and mix well. Add water little by little and knead into a firm dough.
  3. Take a small portion of dough, flatten with your palm or press with a fafda maker on a greased surface.
  4. Gently lift and deep fry in medium hot oil until crisp and golden.
  5. Drain on paper towel and repeat for remaining dough.

Serve With

  • Green chutney
  • Fried green chilies
  • Jalebi
